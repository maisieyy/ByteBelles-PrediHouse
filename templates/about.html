About Us

PrediHouse
Predicting Your Home's Future, Recommending Your Dream

PrediHouse is a website that uses machine learning to predict house prices and recommend properties based on user preferences. It allows users to input criteria like location, price, and features, and uses algorithms to suggest matching properties. The goal is to provide useful information for purchasing decisions. The website has a user-friendly interface for easy navigation and decision-making, aiming to help users make informed choices when searching for their dream home.
